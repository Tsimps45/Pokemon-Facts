All Types of Pokemon

Pokemon are one of many magical species in the anime world and there are 18 types that have been introduced so far. Pokemon Red and Blue (first 2 games created by Gamefreak and Nintendo) introduced the original 151 species of Pokemon, and now with the newest game being being Pokemon Sun and Moon we now have over 721 Species. There have been, so far, 8 generations of pokemon. There are 18 Types of pokemon that are in the entire Pokemon world. The most recognized and sponsered pokemon is... can you guess it ? Thats right, its... Pikachu ! In Pokemon Yellow
Bug: Bug-type Pokemon are characterized by their fast growing and they don't take long to evolve. Bug-type Pokemon live mostly (not all of them) in forests, some of them are a little harder to find because they live atop the trees. There are 25 Bug-type moves.

Bug-type are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Fairy, Fire, Fighting, Flying, Ghost, Poison, and Steel

Bug-type are super effective against pokemon of the types: Dark, Grass, and Psychic
Dark:The Dark- type is represented from traits that are considered feral and untameable, specifically that born from adverse and intense complex social conditions, to that of traits that evoke a negative leaning nature, from having a nocturnal day cycle. exhibitions of unusually cruel, crafty, and clever intelligence. It's hard for a Dark-type Pokemon to form bonds of friendship with its Trainer, but when having one, it will never break, being really faithful to its Trainer and protecting it anytime.

Dark-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Dark, Fairy and Fighting.

Dark-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Ghost and Psychic.
Dragon:The Dragon-type is often considered an ancestral type as many Legendary Dragon-type Pokemon are revered as deities. Other Dragon-type Pokemon are frequently hard to catch and train. They also evolve late and are relatively rare. An interesting fact is that the stats of many Dragon-type Pokemon surpass the stats of other types of Pokemon.

Dragon-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Steel.

Dragon-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Dragon.

Dragon-Types are ineffective against Pokemon of the types: Fairy.
Electric:The Electric-type is a type of Pokemon that have electricity-oriented powers. They possess electro kinetic abilities, being able to control, store, or even produce electricity. Electric-type Pokemon have varied habitats, from forests, praries, cities and power plants. Electric-type Pokemon are usually fast, and many of their attacks may paralyze the target. Some Electric-type Pokemon also resemble artifical objects used by humans that can relate to electricity.

Electric-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Dragon, Electric and Grass.

Electric-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Flying and Water.

Electric-Types are ineffective against Pokemon of the types: Ground.
Fairy:Fairy-type pokemon are generally considered "cute" and tend to be at least partially pink in color and feminine in appearance overall. However, Fairy types can be incredibly powerful. Fairy types also have some magical appeal to them. As opposed to Psychic-type Pokemon, Fairy-type Pokemon have powers related to magic and the supernatural as opposed to mental power.

Fairy-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: poison and steel

Fairy-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: fighting, dragon and dark

Fighting:Pokemon of this type are specialists in melee attacks, such as punches and kicks. It could be said that they're the opposite to Psychic-type Pokemon because while Pokemon of that type depend primarily on special attacks, Pokemon of the Fighting type depend mostly on physical attacks.Most Fighting-type Pokemon have a human-like body shape because martial arts are predominantly practiced by humans. Fighting-type Pokemon have varied ways to evolve as this represents a fighter going on a journey to get stronger and find out who he/she is supposed to be.

Fighting-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Bug, Flying, Poison, Psychic

Fighting-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Dark, Ice, Normal, Rock, Steel

Fighting-Types are ineffective against Pokemon of the types: Ghost
Fire: This one of the most recognized type of Pokemon, since one of the three Starter Pokemon has this type. Fire-type moves are based in attacks of this element, with the control of it and most of them can leave the status Burn, Fire types are also immune to being Burned, regardless of whether is was hit by a super effective move that causes it, such as Scald. They are passionate and some have bad behavior, they live in caves, volcanoes and arid zones. However, there are some other Pokemon that tend to live in green areas.

Fire-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Dragon, Fire, Rock and Water.

Fire-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Bug, Grass, Ice, Steel
Flying:Pokemon of this type can fly, many of them live at high altitudes, even. Most of them are birds and insects. Their power is mostly related with aerial and wind-related attacks. Most of them have wings, but there are also some of them that just float without wings, like Rayquaza and Gyarados. The Flying type is the only type until the moment that is combined with every other Pokemon type, making this type the most varied.

Flying-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Electric, Rock, Steel

Flying-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Bug, Grass, Fighting
Ghost:The Ghost-type is often considered an ancestral type as many Legendary Ghost-type Pokemon are revered as deities. Other Dragon-type Pokemon are frequently hard to catch and train. They also evolve late and are relatively rare. An interesting fact is that the stats of many Dragon-type Pokemon surpass the stats of other types of Pokemon.The Ghost type is notable for having few Pokemon, many of whom have low HP, and moves. Pokémon of this type are usually connected to fear, the dark and the afterlife. They usually live in abandoned houses, cemeteries, funeral places and uninhabited dark places such as caves. Also, Ghost-type Pokemon tend to be extremely naughty, pranking humans just to see their faces and reactions.

Ghost-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Dark

Ghost-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Ghost and Psychic

Ghost-Types are ineffective against Pokemon of the types: Normal
Grass:Grass-type Pokemon are usually calm, pacifistic, and like to take care of flowers, plants and nature itself, but are also great fighters and can poison, paralyze or put targets to sleep. Most of the times, the Pokemon have part of a plant, thus their growth is really similar to that of a plant. Some of them also grow berries, flowers and leaves, and many of them can control their growth. Grass-type Pokemon usually have combinations with the and Poison type. The last one is because many plants are poisonous. The Grass type is the type that has most weaknesses.

Grass-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Bug, Dragon, Fire, Flying, Grass, Poison, Steel

Grass-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Ground, Rock, Water
Ground:Ground-type Pokemon have powers and abilities related to control of ground and earth. Ground-type Pokemon are afraid of water, like Rock-type Pokemon, unless they are Water type. Many Ground Pokemon are also partially Rock type. These Pokemon are normally found in caves or rocky terrains, with the exceptions of some dual typed Pokemon. One bad thing about them is that they can't normally come off the ground, such as Diglett and Dugtrio, even though they are pretty good combatants when they are below the Earth's surface. They have powerful moves such as Earthquake and Fissure.

Ground-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Bug, Grass

Ground-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Electric, Fire, Poison, Rock, Steel

Ground-Types are ineffective against Pokemon of the types: Flying
Ice:Ice-type Pokemon stand out for being able to endure very low temperatures, as well as adapting to freezing weathers. They control ice at will. Their habitats go from the top of mountains, frozen caves and caverns or even the poles. Many Ice-type moves have chances of freezing the target, which prevents them from attacking until they thaw out.

Ice-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Fire, Ice, Steel and Water

Ice-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Dragon, Flying, Grass and Ground
Normal:The Normal type covers most of the Pokemon moves than the rest of the types. Most of them are harmless, or don't do it directly, they affect the Pokemon's characteristics instead. However, the damaging moves are based on standard attacks, like Tackle and Scratch. Normal-type Pokemon are more varied, with various statistics, growths and evolution methods. They also differ in habitats, they can be found in forests, caves, mountains, cities, etc. This type is the one with less weaknesses, along with the Electric type. But this type is also the only one that isn't very effective against another one.

Normal-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Rock and Steel

Normal-Types are ineffective against Pokemon of the types: Ghost
Poison:These Pokemon have a natural toxic quality, maybe they produce venom, are poisonous plants, or even represent pollution. They normally live in caves, marshes or similar places. A very curious fact about Poison-type Pokemon is that some of them resemble ninjas. For example, Koffing is like a smoke ball, Crobat has a ninja's speed and secrecy and Toxicroak has paws that make it resemble a ninja.The Poison type is related to the status effects Poisoned and Badly Poisoned, which are produced mostly by Poison-type moves.

Poison-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Bug, Ghost, Ground, Poison and Rock

Poison-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Fairy and Grass

Poison-Types are ineffective against Pokemon of the types: Steel
Psychic:Many Pokemon are of this type, and, being the type with most Legendary Pokemon, for many, the Psychic type is the most powerful. Psychic-type Pokémon tend to be very intelligent.To attack, they use their mind power, thus having high special stats and low physical stats. However, this is not always like this.

Psychic-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Psychic and Steel

Psychic-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Fighting and Poison

Psychic-Types are ineffective against Pokemon of the types: Dark
Rock:Rock-type Pokemon stand out for their great defense to physical attacks, however, this type is tied with the Grass-type as having the most weaknesses, with five, and Pokémon of this type are not very fast. Also, some Rock-type moves don't have great accuracy.All Fossil Pokémon are of this type. Their fossils preserve enough material genetic to be worked with to bring them back to life, but, it can be known, that the Rock type was very abundant. They also have unique combinations to Pokémon of the present.The Rock type is commonly combined with the Ground type, so they are usually confused, although they're not the same. Even in the anime this confusion has been present, saying that Rock-type Pokemon are immune to Electric-type attacks.

Rock-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Fighting, Ground and Steel

Rock-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: bug, Fire, Flying and Ice
Steel:It was introduced in Generation II, along with the Dark type, to balance the other types. It was added to the typing of two Pokémon, Magnemite and Magneton, which were only Electric-type.Steel-type Pokémon stand out for having the best defense. They tend to be heavy and thus have low Speed. Steel-types are mainly inorganic in nature, some of them representing robots and machines. Along with the Rock and Ground types, Steel represents part of the Earth's minerals as types.

Steel-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Electric, Fire, Steel and Water

Steel-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Fairy, Ice and Rock
Water:It is said that this type is pure since its Pokemon can adapt to any situation and weather, since water can adapt itself in several forms. For example, water adapts the form of a glass if poured into one, while if poured in a cube it adopts the form of the cube. That's why Water-type Pokemon feel happy anywhere, as long as there's water. Most Pokémon of this type also have another type, representing the biodiversity of marine creatures. Most Water-type Pokemon are based on creatures that live on water, or use water for their disposition. Their attacks involve use of water, if not, attacks that can be done only by marine creatures (like Clamp, Crabhammer and Razor Shell).

Water-Types are not very effective against Pokemon of the types: Dragon, Grass and Water

Water-Types are super effective against Pokemon of the types: Fire, Ground and Rock
